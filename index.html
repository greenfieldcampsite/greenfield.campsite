About Us

Opened in 2025 with modern facilities, Greenfield Campsite is located in a quiet, cozy part of Ulcinj, just 550 meters from Velika Plaža beach, along a shaded trail where you might spot turtles and taste wild blackberries. The site features a spacious illuminated area for campervans, tents, games, and peaceful relaxation. Guests enjoy clean showers and toilets, fresh water supply, wastewater disposal, barbecue facilities, and a play area. Pets are welcome. Nearby are cafés, grocery shops, and fruit stands. Children can also play at a nearby hotel playground with swings and a football goal.
